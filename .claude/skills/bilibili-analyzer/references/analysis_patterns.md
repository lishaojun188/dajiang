# Bilibili Video Analysis Patterns

## Engagement Metrics Interpretation

### Like Ratio Quality Scale
- >90%: Exceptional content (viral potential)
- 80-90%: High quality content
- 70-80%: Above average
- 60-70%: Average
- <60%: Below average (controversial or low quality)

### Comment-to-View Ratio Indicators
- >5%: Highly engaging content
- 2-5%: Good engagement
- 1-2%: Average engagement
- <1%: Low engagement

### Upload Frequency Analysis
- Daily uploads: >20 videos/month
- Regular: 4-20 videos/month
- Occasional: 1-4 videos/month
- Rare: <1 video/month

## Content Quality Assessment

### Technical Quality Indicators
- **Resolution**: 1080p, 2K, 4K
- **Bitrate**: Higher values indicate better quality
- **FPS**: 24, 30, 60 fps
- **Codec**: Modern codecs (H.264, H.265) vs older (AV1)

### Production Value Indicators
- Professional thumbnails
- High contrast/color balance
- Compressed/framed shots
- Good lighting
- Clear audio quality

## Trend Identification

### Keywords in Popular Videos
- Check title and description for trending terms
- Compare with similar videos in category
- Look for seasonal/event-based patterns

### Growth Trajectory Patterns
- **Linear Growth**: Steady increase in views over time (standard content)
- **Viral Growth**: Sharp spike followed by decay (trending/controversial)
- **Slow Burn**: Gradual growth over weeks/months (evergreen content)
- **Spike and Plateau**: High initial growth, then sustained level

## UP主 Analysis Approach

### Channel Assessment Factors
1. **Content Consistency**: Topic focus vs variety
2. **Upload Frequency**: How often new content is added
3. **Audience Retention**: Repeat viewers vs new viewers
4. **Interaction Quality**: Response to comments, community events
5. **Cross-Platform Presence**: Links to other social media

### Red Flags in UP主 Profiles
- Sudden drop in upload frequency
- Excessive removed/deleted videos
- Compaints about copyright or policy violations
- Large number of videos with low view counts

## Content Safety Checklist

### Policy Compliance Review
- Check for age-restricted content markers
- Look for sensitive content warnings
- Verify content matches declared tags/category
- Check comment moderation approach

### Intellectual Property Considerations
- Verify music/video assets are properly licensed
- Check for unauthorized re-uploads
- Look for content scraping/reupload patterns
